The Solution

We strive to get young ballplayers to a game near them. We have people working all around the country to help get tickets to MLB games from all 30 teams. If you live in a state that doesn't have a team, we can still get you tickets to the closest game. Feel free to email us or fill out the contact sheet below. There may be a wait from when you contact us to the date of a game.
