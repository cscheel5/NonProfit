The Problem

Many young ballplayers dream of seeing their favorite MLB stars play in person. Unfortunately, many of these kids are unable to witness these players in real life. Witnessing an MLB game is person will change a young kids life. Not only will they fall in love with the game, they will dream. One game of proffesional baseball holds an insane amount of power and inspiration for kids.


Reasons Kids Can't Attend

  • Parents cannot afford a trip to a game
  • Parents are too busy for a game
  • Parents can't transport them
  • There is no games near their home
